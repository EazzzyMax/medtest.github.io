# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Russian-speaking medical students and clinicians preparing for primary accreditation who need to find a specific test question or clinical case quickly while studying.

## Product Purpose

Provide a fast, local-first searchable browser for the MedikTest question bank and clinical cases. Success means users can move between questions and cases, narrow by topic, tolerate minor query mistakes, and immediately see the relevant answers and explanations without navigating the original training application.

## Brand Personality

Focused, familiar, dependable. The interface should feel like a serious study tool: dense enough for repeated use, calm enough for long reading sessions, and direct in its language.

## Anti-references

Do not turn the tool into a marketing page, a decorative medical dashboard, or a card-heavy mobile mockup. Avoid hiding core study content behind unfamiliar controls or changing the established question-search behavior.

## Design Principles

- Preserve search behavior and interaction patterns across questions and clinical cases.
- Put retrieval speed and readable medical content ahead of decoration.
- Reveal the full clinical scenario progressively without losing context.
- Keep exact phrase matches visibly ahead of fuzzy word matches.
- Make desktop study efficient while retaining a complete mobile workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain keyboard focus visibility, semantic controls, readable contrast, selectable text, reduced-motion support, and layouts that remain usable from 320px wide screens upward.
